# Product

## Register

brand

## Users

High-net-worth individuals and corporate executives looking for elite, strategic asset management and cross-border financial advisory.

## Product Purpose

MMG Bank is a strategic partner in financial advisory, designed to accompany clients through every stage of their evolution and expansion. With a strong regional presence in Panama, the Bahamas, and Puerto Rico, the institution serves as a trusted ally supporting international growth.

As dedicated asset managers, MMG Bank focuses on optimizing portfolios and safeguarding capital through robust, well-diversified strategies tailored to meet unique financial goals and protect wealth over the long term.

### Core Solutions
- **Investment Solutions**: Tailored strategies designed to maximize returns while managing risk.
- **Banking Services**: Premium banking infrastructure to support day-to-day and long-term financial needs.
- **Corporate & Strategic Advisory**: Expert guidance to navigate complex business landscapes and accelerate corporate expansion.

## Brand Personality

Prestigious, Trustworthy, Strategic (Elite, calm, high-end professional feel)

## Anti-references

- Generic corporate blue layouts
- Cliché stock photography (anonymous handshakes/skyscrapers)
- Over-rounded card styling (border-radius >= 24px) or sketchy doodle-style illustrations
- Excessive or low-performance decorative animations

## Design Principles

- **Expert Confidence**: Present financial insights and structures with clean, authoritative, well-balanced Bornia typography and generous whitespace that instills trust and security.
- **Clarity in Complexity**: Break down sophisticated banking services and asset management strategies into clear, structured, and interactive sections.
- **Strategic Regional Connectivity**: Highlight regional operations (Panama, Bahamas, Puerto Rico) cohesively, emphasizing MMG Bank’s global and borderless presence.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

- Target WCAG 2.1 Level AA conformance for contrast, text sizing, and accessibility.
- Support options for reduced motion (`prefers-reduced-motion`) on page-level transitions.
- Fully semantic HTML structure to ensure ease of navigation for assistive screen readers.
